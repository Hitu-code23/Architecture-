01
The Studio

A practice of restraint, composed at the threshold of mass and light.

Founded in 2009 between Tokyo and Copenhagen, Aether is led by twelve architects working at the intersection of phenomenology and digital craft. Every commission begins with a year of listening — to site, season and client — before the first line is drawn.

Philosophy

"We do not build buildings. We choreograph the way light, weight and breath meet inside a room."

— Sora Inoue, Founding Partner
I

Material truth

Concrete, brass, stone — left to speak in their own register.

II

Light as program

We design for the sun first; the brief comes second.

III

Quiet surfaces

Detail withdrawn until only proportion remains.

IV

Climate intelligence

Every wall is a thermodynamic instrument.
